Heidegger’s Language in the Poem

28 May 2019 — reading-notes — heidegger

[ONGOING]

My reading group comprises three others and myself, separated by three successive time zones. We’re meeting once weekly via Skype to arrive at an understanding on a sentence-by-sentence basis. It’s good work in the best company.

28 May 2019

Our first meeting was last Tuesday night, and I was completely unprepared. Over dinner with one of the group’s members, I’d begged my way in when he’d mentioned the upcoming weekly discussion, but I didn’t pay close enough attention to the essay’s title. All I recalled was “the way to language” — so astray I went. What follows initially is what I might have contributed if I’d been able to do more than take an occasional bite from a sandwich.
