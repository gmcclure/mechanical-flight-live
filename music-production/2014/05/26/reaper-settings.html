Reaper Settings for Mac OS X

26 May 2014 — music-production — reaper

[DRAFT]

I like Reaper. It’s funky – the most finished unfinished piece of software I’ve encountered. A basic installation, however, is not very usable, or at least not pleasantly so. These are my preferences.

Arrangement

The very first thing I do is right-click the transport and change Docked transport position to Top of main window.

Shortcuts

Action Shortcut Notes
Quit Cmd-Q The basic quit default.
Toggle Fullscreen Cmd-Ctrl-F The usual macOS fullscreen toggle.
View: Scroll horizantally reversed HorizWheel (Trackpad horizontal drag) My preferred scroll setting.
Undo Z  
Cut (focus-dependent, within time selection) X
